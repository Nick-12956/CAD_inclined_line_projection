# CAD_inclined_line_projection
AutoCAD auto drawing of inclined line projection (First angle projection)  
IT WORKS FOR 4 DIFFERENT CASES  
1. inclined to HP  
2. inclined to VP
3. inclined to HP and on the VP
4. inclined to VP and on the HP

Sample questions for all four cases-  
1. A line AB of length 75mm is making an inclination at 30° to HP and is parallel to the VP. Its end A is 25 mm above HP and 30 mm infront of VP. Draw its projections.
2. A line AB of length 75mm is making an inclination at 30° to VP and is parallel to the HP. Its end B is 25 mm infront of VP and 30 mm above HP. Draw its projections.
3. A line CD of length 50mm is inclined at 60° to HP and parallel to VP. Draw its projection when point end C is 30mm above HP and in the VP.
4. A line CD of length 50mm is inclined at 60° to VP and parallel to HP. Draw its projection when point end D is 30mm infront of VP and in the HP.

CAN ALSO CHANGE VALUES OF DISTANCE AND ANGLE, BUT QUESTION PATTERN SHOULD BE SAME.
